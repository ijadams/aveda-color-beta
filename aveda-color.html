Which Color is right for you?
Our expert stylists can help you choose.

MAKE A PERMANENT CHANGE

Full Spectrum™ Permanent Hair Color can take you lighter, darker, or off the natural spectrum.

  • Fade-resistant
  • Available in any shade you can dream up
  • Up to 96% naturally-derived*
  • Essentially damage-free
  • Contains a signature blend of botanical oils (sunflower, castor and jojoba)

ENHANCE YOUR NATURAL COLOR

Enhance your existing hair color or blend your greys with Full Spectrum™ Deposit-Only Color Treatment.

  • Low maintenance; low commitment
  • Lasts up to 8 weeks
  • Up to 99% naturally-derived*
  • Actually improves the condition of damaged hair

LIFT & TONE DARK HAIR

Full Spectrum Deep™ is designed to provide lasting, cool-toned color for naturally dark hair.

  • Lifts and tones dark, coarse hair
  • Up to 93% naturally-derived*
  • Contains a signature blend of botanical oils (sunflower, castor and jojoba)

GO BLONDE

Achieve lighter color with Enlightener Blonding Service.

  • Protects with a signature blend of botanical oils (sunflower, castor and jojoba)
  • Features the Pure-Fume aroma of rose, lemon, peppermint and other pure flower and plant essences
*From plants, non-petroleum minerals or water. Learn more at aveda.com/hair_color
